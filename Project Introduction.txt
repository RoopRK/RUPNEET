                                                   Live Safe,Live Smart

The name of our project is "Live Safe,Live Smart", we are going to design an application in which we will be
implementing something that is going to help the society especially women of our society, this application will
update the current location of the person to the designated contacts whenever the person wants to do so, or feels
like they are in any problem or not safe. Even if the internet is not available it will work offline.
